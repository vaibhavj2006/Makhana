Who's behind this

A snack brand built around one weird, wonderful seed.

How it started

A kitchen staple nobody outside India had heard of.

Makhana has been roasted in Indian kitchens for generations — used in curries, desserts, and fasting-day meals. But on supermarket shelves, it was either unbranded and inconsistent, or dressed up as a "superfood" at ten times the price it should cost.

Pond & Puff started with a simple bet: treat fox nuts like a real snack brand would — consistent roasting, honest flavors, and packaging that doesn't feel like a lecture on wellness.

What we won't do

No filler, no vague sourcing, no false "healthy" labels.

We list every ingredient because there usually aren't many. We roast instead of fry because that's genuinely how makhana is best made — not because it sounds good on a label. And we don't call ourselves a superfood company, because we're a snack company that happens to sell something good for you.
